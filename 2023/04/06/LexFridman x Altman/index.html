Podcast_Lex Fridman x Sam Altman

发表于2023-04-06|更新于2023-06-10|Foundation
|字数总计:248|阅读时长:1分钟|阅读量:

by topic

  1. The phase of AGI

    • GPT 4 is early AI like early computer, slow, buggy.
  1. How the breakthrough happened
    • In early stage, GPT is not useful. Until we introduce human feedback reinforcement learning, ask the GPT which answer is better, and adopt human’s choose, which makes the model useful.

  1. What is the most amazing part?

    • It’s remarkble that you can use it as a reasoning engine in some definitions.
    • In the early future, it’s the most impactful tool that will benefit how people work.
  1. About competition
    • People got caught up in the parameters count race.
    • As for competition, there will be lots of AGI in the world. GPT is not the only one. OpenAI is one of the teams.
  1. About Elon
    • About Elon, Sam agrees on the downside of AI, disagree on the attck from Elon on twitter.
    • Without Elon, the world cannot make progress so rapidly.
  1. Which job might be affected most?
    • full end to end job better
    • Customer service will have fewer jobs very soon. GPT models will make lots of jobs go away. Every technological revolutions does.

  1. The impact of economy

    • The cost of intelligent and the cost of energy will down

      -> which will lead to economic output larger

      -> the whole society will be richer.

  1. Advice to young people
    • keep internal driven, independent thinking, don’t listen other’s advice.
    • Introspect make Sam find the meaning of life.
